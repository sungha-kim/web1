HTML elements are the building blocks of HTML pages. with HTML constructs, images and other objects, such as interactive forms, may be embedded into the rendered pafe. it provides a means to creaate structured documents by denoting structural semantics for text such as headings, paragraphs, lists, links, quotes and other items.
